DATA COMPARISON

Operators

== and === > The first operator compares values, the second compares including the data type
// 10 == '10' true; 10 === '10' false.

!= and !== > Are used to check the inequality of two values, they work in the same way as == and ===.
// 5 ! = '5' false 5 !== '5' true

>,<,>=,<=   These work just like in math
